The Old Farmer and His Lazy Sons Once there lived a wealthy old farmer in a village. He was a very hardworking person. His determination and hard work made him a wealthy person. With great toil and sweat he earned many lands and properties. He had four sons. They were leading a very lazy life. They were strong and healthy, but very indifferent to the reality of life. They were good and did not quarrel among themselves, but they did not want to work. The farmer was very worried about the fact. The farmer was getting older and sick. But his sons were still leading an aimless and lazy life. He was thinking about how to teach his sons the importance of hard work. He wanted his sons to take care of his lands. So, he made a plan. One day he called his sons to his bed and told them that he was very sick and he might die at any time. He had kept a great treasure under his land so that his sons would never go hungry. After his death, they should dig the land and bring out the wealth. After a few days the old man died. The sons became sad for some days. Then the sons planned to dig the lands to get the hidden treasure. So, they took their father’s spades and dug every portion of their field in search of the hidden treasure. But they found nothing. They became very frustrated. Some wise villagers suggested that they should sow some seeds as they have already dug their fields. They accepted the suggestion and spread some seeds on the land. That year it rained well. After a few days the plants started to grow from the seeds. They took proper care of the plants. As a result, they harvested a huge amount of crops. So, they understood well what their father wanted to teach them. And they changed themselves and started working hard.
